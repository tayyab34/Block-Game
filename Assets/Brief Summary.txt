                                             Block Game 

  In this game, player has to avoid obstacles and collect coins. 1 coin gives 1 score. When obstacle collide
  with player 1 live reduce. Total lives are 3. When live=0, Game is over. 

  Steps: 

  1) Player and its movements 
      Use transform.translate for movement and set boundary.									 
  2) Obstacle and Spawning
    create prefab of 1 obstacle by combining four cubes.
    spawn using instantiate. 	
  3) Coins spawned using instantiate.
  4) Lives and score updated in game manager class.

  Faced issue in spawning of obstacle.
  I enjoyed spawning of obstacle because it gives me great learning.
